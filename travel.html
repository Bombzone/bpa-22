You can reduce your carbon emissions by driving less, whether this is walking, biking or public transport, you can also reduce how much you drive by taking fewer trips, and getting more errands done at one time.

For your car you would want to keep tires properly inflated, and make sure that if your city requires it your car passes emissions/inspection and is in proper operable condition.

The way you drive also affects how much fuel, money and carbon you emmit, so keeping your foot out of it absolutely reduces your carbon emissions and footprint.

Use waze or google maps to avoid traffic jams and find the most efficient route.

You can consider electric or partially electric vehicles.
