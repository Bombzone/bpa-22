As a community you can cut back on your plastic emissions by using refillable water bottles or totally abandoning plastic water bottles entirely. Doing this will reduce the emissions used to make said plastic water bottles, and keep them from ending up in landfills.

Reuse items and if they have no use to you they might to someone else, donate them or repurpose them, keep them out of the land fill.

Instead of allowing your food waste to go into the landfill you can compost it, by doing this you can reduce the emissions it produces when it decomposes in the landfill.

Try to buy used clothes rather than new ones that take energy and produce emissions when they are being produced.

Use reusable shopping bags

Save water by using plumbing appliances that use less water and are more efficient.

Recycle recyclable items whenever possible.
