You can as a community reduce your carbon emissions by turning off lights when they are not needed, replacing halogen and CFL (fluorescent bulbs) with Leds, they are up to 90% more efficient and last much longer, which reduces waste.

Another thing you can partake in is unplugging devices when they are not in use as such devices use some amount of standby power, which does add up.

When washing clothes using cold water reduces energy spent to heat water, and is just as effective with the right detergent.

By keeping your home a few degrees cooler in the winter and a little warmer in the summer it will take less energy to heat and cool your home, and save you money in the process.

If possible, use renewable energy, and purchase/use items that are rated to use less energy.
